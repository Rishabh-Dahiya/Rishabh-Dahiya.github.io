About Dentist Department

The vaccination of dentists against various vaccine-preventable infections is the most important meaasure of primary prevention against such diseases which would otherwise prove fatal for both the dentists and their patients

  • CF-related diabetes (monthly)
  • Gastrointestinal (monthly)
  • CF-related liver disease (monthly)
  • Sweat testing (three times per month)
  • Central Nervous Systems
    Open visitation with only two visitors in a room at any time. No one under 12 years of age may.
  • Allergic Disorders
    Open visitation with only two visitors in a room at any time. No one under 12 years of age may.
  • Neoplastic Disorders
    Open visitation with only two visitors in a room at any time. No one under 12 years of age may.
